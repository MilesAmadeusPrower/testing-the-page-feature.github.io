/t found (name)

If you are unranked, you are able to found a new town in your nation's (or state's) territory. Take note that if your nation is one that wants approval before founding, it may be a good idea to seek approval first.

/t disband

Disbands your town and returns everything to your liege

/t rename (new name)

Renames your town to something else.

/t deposit (#)

Deposits gold to your town bank

/t withdraw (#)

Withdraws gold from your town bank

/t setspawn

Sets your town's spawn point

/t spawn

Goes to your town's spawn point

/t remove (player)

Evicts a player from town.

/t add (player)

Adds a player to town.

/t claim

Integrates a chunk from your liege's territory to your own. Cannot be used in wilderness.

/t unclaim

Returns a chunk to your liege's territory.

/t info

Prints information about your town.
