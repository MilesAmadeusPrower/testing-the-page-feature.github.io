Priorities has left guilds such as this put on the back burner. For this reason, this section is mostly for debug purposes.

/uni new (name)

Creates a new school in which the founder/teachers can use as a medium to build reports of skillsets the player in question has. Because this is dependent entirely on player-run benevolence, it should go without saying that it is unwise to rely on unreliable sources of information, and only schools who have earned their credibility and done proper work before building their reports should be used.

/uni info (name)

Creates a new school in which the founder/teacher can use as a medium to build reports of skillsets the player in question has. Because this is dependent entirely on player-run benevolence, it is unwise to rely on unreliable sources of information.
