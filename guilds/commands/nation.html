/n new (name)

Tags: create, new, build

Create a new nation from scratch. This is the first command for anyone interested in building their own domain

/n apply (nation)

Tags: recruit, apply, join, add

Apply to join another nation that you are interested in. This will notify all players that are online in that nation.

/n chat

Tags: radio, join

Set your chatting medium to this channel.

/n spawn

Tags: goto, spawn, warp

Go to your nation's spawn

/n claim

Tags: claim, build, territory

Annex an unclaimed chunk into your nation

/n setspawn

Tags: goto, build, spawn, warp

Set your nation's spawn point to the place in which you stand.

/n integrate (subject)

Tags: claim, build, lord, integrate, subjects

Take a chunk that you own, and annex it into your subject

/n unclaim

Tags: claim, build

Revoke a claimed chunk of territory and return it to mother nature.

/n disband

Tags: destroy, disband, leave

Disbands your nation. Warning: This is a potentially catastrophic command to use, and should not be considered lightly. All states within your nation will become independent, and all capital demesne will be returned to mother nature, and be exposed to raiders.

/n rename (new name)

Tags: build, change, create

Changes your country to a different name. This may be useful if a spelling error was made, a dynasty was changed, or a nation-wide decision was concluded.

/n delstate (state)

Tags: destroy, disband

Deletes a subject state and returns every asset to its liege. This is a very unstable action to take, and can even destroy the mandate of your country.

/n deltown (town)

Tags: destroy, disband

Deletes a town and returns every asset to the nation's jurisdiction. This can be unstable depending on the town's power base.

/n addstate (owner) (state)

Tags: subjects, lord, build

Creates a new state with the player as its lord, and its first chunk where the player stands. Take note that the lord must be ranked as an officer or similar prior to granting.

/n addtown (owner) (town)

Tags: subjects, build

Creates a new town where the player stands. and adds it to the nation's demesne. Any player is able to be used so long as they are part of the nation

/n coronate (player)

Tags: leave, disband, heir

This will abdicate the leader, and grant leadership to the new player. The new player must be ranked as a successor prior to use.

/n deposit (#)

Tags: bank

Deposit money into your national bank.

/n withdraw (#)

Tags: bank

Get money from your national bank. Must be leader to use this command!

/n info [nation]

Tags: info, list, nation

Use this to get info on yourself, or another nation.

/n verbose [nation]

Tags: info, list, nation

Use this to get info on yourself, or another nation, but in a way that is really verbose, chat clogging, and recommended more so for debug purposes.

/n projection

Tags: info, nation

Get your mandate losses and gains expected by the end of the 72 minecraft day cycle. Immediate actions are not shown on this.

/n list

Tags: list, nation

Lists all countries on the entire map.

/n shares

Tags: nation

This will show how much of nation land is part of every state as well as the nation land.

/n color #RRGGBB

Tags: nation, build

Changes the colour of your country on the map

/n remove (player)

Tags: destroy, evict, exile, sack

Revokes a player from being part of your nation, effectively putting them into exile.

/n add (player)

Tags: recruit, add, join

If the player has applied, they will join instantly. If the player has not applied, they will be sent an invite to join. Whether they join or not is on their own volition.

/n applicants

Tags: recruit

Gets all of the players that have asked to join your nation. To accept an invitation, type /n add (applicant).

/n join (nation)

Tags: recruit, add, join

Joins the given nation of which you were invited to.

/n appoint (rank) (player)

Tags: add, build

Gives duties and powers to a player in your nation.

/n addheir (player)

Tags: add, build

Designates the selected player as a successor - this is a shortcut command to /n appoint heir (player)

/n regent (player)

Tags: add, build

Designates the selected player as a regent - this is a shortcut command to /n appoint regent (player)

/n sack (player)

Tags: destroy, evict, exile, sack

Revokes a player's duties, or powers. Players with more power will cost the nation's power more. A player with a powerful enough rank will be able to make a nation unable to sack them.
