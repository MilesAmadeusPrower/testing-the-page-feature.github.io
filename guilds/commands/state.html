/s claim

Integrates a chunk from your owner's territory onto yours.

/s unclaim

Returns a chunk of your territory back to your owner

/s form

Turn your independent state into a nation, in event your nation was disbanded.

/s addtown (town) (player)

Builds a new town with the player as mayor, and its first chunk claimed where the player stands.

/s rename (name)

Changes your state's name

/s deposit (#)

Deposits gold into your state's bank

/s withdraw (#)

/s addheir (player)

A shortcut for /s appoint heir (player)

/s disband

Disbands the state and returns all assets to its owner

/s abdicate

Abdicates your position as lord, and automatically succeeds another.

/s add (player)

Sends an invite to a player to join your state.

/s remove (player)

Removes the player from your state (if they are not part of a town).

/s setspawn

Changes the spawn point of your state.

/s spawn

Goes to your state's spawn point

/s info [state]

Prints information about the selected state.
